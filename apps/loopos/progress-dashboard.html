Active Goal · Integrated Acceptance and Real-Team Trial

V6-M6 is the single active Goal. It proves a real team can complete setup, activate, and run one weekly tension-to-closure operating loop with human accountability and AI assistance.

Required evidence

  • First-run setup, activation, role assignment, goal cycle, tactical meeting, and governance meeting are browser-proven
  • Organization Brain supports the trial path without bypassing human authority
  • Candidate tensions remain evidence-backed and require authorized human confirmation
  • Production deployment, health/readiness, rollback, and authenticated smoke evidence are separated from local proof
  • Production Reader and BioCoach data isolation remain invariant
  • Longitudinal real-team completion is not claimed before evidence is collected

Current engineering gate

V6-M1, V6-M2, V6-M3, V6-M4, and V6-M5 are accepted after independent reviews. V6-M6 is active. M6-A contract and evidence harness are accepted. M6-B proves a local SQL-seeded fixture browser path with tactical/governance outcomes, focused governance regression tests, final production build, and independent reclosures. M6-C is accepted as local fixture evidence. M6-D is accepted as local UI-first setup evidence after full verifier, implementation review reclosure, and roadmap/evidence reclosure. M6-E has deployed the corrected Aliyun release with public web health, authenticated smoke, and BioCoach isolation passing after one rollback from a bad package layout. Independent reviews and real-team longitudinal use remain open.
